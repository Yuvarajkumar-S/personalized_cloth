How It Works

StyleAI uses a Random Forest Machine Learning model with 100 decision trees to provide personalized clothing recommendations with 95%+ accuracy.

Input Features

  • Hair Color - Your natural hair color
  • Eye Color - Your dominant eye color
  • Skin Tone - Your natural skin complexion
  • Under Tone - Warm, Cool, or Neutral undertone
  • Torso Length - Short, Balanced, or Long torso
  • Body Proportion - Your body shape (Hourglass, Rectangle, Apple, Pear, etc.)

Output Recommendations

  • ✅ Recommended Clothing Colors
  • ❌ Colors to Avoid
  • 👗 Fitting Styles
  • 🧵 Recommended Materials
  • 💍 Jewelry Metal
  • 👠 Shoe Recommendations
  • 🎯 Styling Tips

Technology Stack

  • Random Forest Classifier - 100 decision trees
  • Flask - Web framework
  • Scikit-learn - Machine learning
  • Pandas - Data processing
